Single Shot Face Swap Demonstration

The video clip below shows the faces of the panelist swapped onto the same video of a single panelist. A "one-shot" technique requires only a single image to do the face swap. In this case the relatively low resolution head shots of the panelilst from the online event announcement were used.

Barbie, Oppenheimer, and Jimmy

"Barbie, Oppenheimer, and Jimmy" is a satirical take on utopia's, as portrayed in Barbie, and on dystopian visions as portrayed in Oppenheimer. It tells this tale using AI tools to face swap all the main characters in Barbie and Oppenheimer with that of the video commentator. Both films are worldwide cultural sensations and are linked together. At the same time AI technologies have risen to the notice of the general public. This video use worldwide recognizable characters in a satirical commentary on contemporary culture, current events, and the potential for it all to disappear. It pokes fun at the utopian and often over the top content of Barbie with the dramatic and potential civilization ending content covered in Oppenheimer. AI could turn out to be as harmless as a fun summer movie or it may threaten the existence of humanity.

In a blink of an eye this face swapping technique looks antiquated yet is remarkable at its time. The more "AI" assisted tools develop the harder it will be to distinguish reality from fiction.

Large Language Models

The videos below show the use of Large Language Models or LLMs to write code, recommendation letters, and resumes. Current LLMs still need a human to edit and polish the output to make it useable but that is only a temporary situation. As the number of parameters in the models continues to explode, there will be little to no need for human intervention.
