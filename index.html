Art of Leadership Panel: Jimmy Kuehnle Notes

"Barbie, Oppenheimer, and Jimmy" tells a tale of face swapping with all the main characters in the summer cultural sensations of Barbie and Oppenheimer is a satirical take on utopia's, as portrayed in Barbie, and in distopia portrayed in Oppenheimer. AI technologies could help or cause great harm in the wrong hands. Which will it be? In a blink of an eye this face swapping technique looks antiquated yet is remarkable at its time. The more "AI" assisted tools develop the harder it will be to distinguish reality from fiction. For now, using tools like Roop is fun and comical. Hopefully a new dawn of creation will be born along with the inevitable plague of misinformation. Until that joyful or dystopian future, enjoy Jimmy's face swapped onto Barbie and Oppenheimer in this funny video.
